Somnia

Somnia is a side-scroller Android game which follows the story of a protagonist trapped in his dream. The protagonist is represented as a shadow that searches around an abandoned house to collect his memories and eventually solve his identity.

Somnia Home Page

Screenshot when Player starts the game

This project was submitted for Software Engineering and Android class. This was a group project. Unfortunately, due to time constraint, the project was abandoned. The protagonist will remain in the shadows, unable to solve the mystery of his existence.
